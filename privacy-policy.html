Privacy Policy

Hisaab Sathi stores your ledger data locally on your device by default. If you choose to log in, cloud sync may store your finance entries in the shared family Firebase project under your account.

Backup export to Gmail or JSON is initiated by you. You control when a backup is created, shared, restored, or deleted.

Notification preferences are stored locally in your browser. Feedback messages are sent through your email app when you choose to send them.

If you want your stored cloud data removed, contact the project owner using the contact page.
