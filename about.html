About HS

Hisaab Sathi

Hisaab Sathi is the personal finance ledger app in the Aapka-Sathi family. It is designed for fast daily use on mobile so you can log expenses, earnings, and udhar without friction.

HS keeps three questions clear at all times: kitna kharch hua, kitna kamaya, aur kitna paisa abhi bhi dusron ke paas udhar me pada hai.
